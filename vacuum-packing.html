What is Vacuum Packing?

Vacuum packing is a method of packaging that removes air from the package before sealing. By eliminating oxygen — the primary driver of spoilage — this technique creates an oxygen-free environment that significantly slows the growth of bacteria, yeast, and mould.

At DeHyd Naturals, we use commercial-grade vacuum sealing equipment to package a wide range of food products securely and hygienically, making them ready for retail, storage, or export.

Key Benefits

  • Extends shelf life up to 3–5 times compared to conventional packaging
  • Prevents freezer burn in frozen products
  • Retains flavour, colour, and nutritional value
  • Reduces food waste and storage costs
  • Tamper-evident, airtight seal builds consumer trust
  • Suitable for retail, bulk distribution, and export

Our Process

How Vacuum Packing Works

A clean, consistent 4-step process ensures every package meets our quality standard.

Step 01

Inspection & Preparation

Products are inspected, sorted, portioned, and cleaned to meet strict hygiene standards before processing begins.

Step 02

Loading

Items are carefully placed into food-grade vacuum pouches of the appropriate size for a snug, secure fit.

Step 03

Air Extraction

Our commercial vacuum machines remove 99.9 % of air from the pouch, creating a near-perfect vacuum environment.

Step 04

Heat Sealing

The pouch is heat-sealed to create an airtight bond, then labelled and prepared for storage or dispatch.
