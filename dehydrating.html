Controlled moisture removal that concentrates flavour, preserves nutrients, and gives your food a dramatically longer shelf life.

What is Food Dehydration?

Food dehydration is the process of removing water content from food using controlled temperature and airflow. Without moisture, microorganisms cannot survive, which naturally preserves the food without any chemical additives or preservatives.

At Shailee Foods, we use precision-controlled dehydrating equipment to ensure uniform drying at optimal temperatures — protecting the natural colour, aroma, nutrients, and taste of every product we process.

Key Benefits

  • Shelf life extended to months or even years without refrigeration
  • Preserves vitamins, minerals, and natural enzymes
  • No chemical preservatives required — 100% natural
  • Significantly reduces weight and volume for easy storage and shipping
  • Intensifies natural flavour and aroma
  • Ideal for retail, food service, exports, and home use

Our Process

Our Dehydration Process

A careful, step-by-step approach to ensure consistent quality and food safety in every batch.

Step 01

Sourcing & Inspection

Raw materials are sourced fresh and inspected thoroughly for quality, ripeness, and hygiene before processing.

Step 02

Washing & Preparation

Products are washed, peeled, sliced, or blanched to the required specifications for even and efficient drying.

Step 03

Controlled Drying

Items are placed in our dehydrating chambers at precise temperature and humidity settings for optimal moisture removal.

Step 04

Quality Check & Packing

Finished products are tested for moisture content, colour, and aroma before being packed, labelled, and dispatched.
